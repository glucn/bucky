# Requirements: Account Grouping (F-004)

## Summary

Allow users to organize accounts into ordered groups, with consistent display and management across user and category accounts.

## User Goals

- Create, rename, reorder, and delete account groups.
- Move accounts into and out of groups.
- Preserve group order across sessions.

## Functional Requirements

- Support CRUD operations for account groups.
- Provide display ordering for groups.
- Support grouping for user accounts and category accounts.
- Ensure group order updates are persisted.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Group management should be fast and consistent in the UI.
- Group operations should be transactional and safe.
